The Letterettes are four gal pals who share a love of letterforms, four-legged creatures and, most importantly, sequined jackets.

We provide a range of lettering services, including creative, signage, murals, and our most coveted service: on-the-spot hand-lettered artwork at promotional pop-ups, grand openings, special occasions and conferences.

We’ve been featured in The Design Files, Frankie Magazine and Collective Hub, and have created work for Disney, Who Gives A Crap, Mimco, Westfield and more.
